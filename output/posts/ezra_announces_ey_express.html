Ezra Announces Engine Yard Express VM Image

A Slice of a Slice

2008-06-01

Along with Vertebra, which isn't available for use just yet, Ezra announced Engine Yard Express. Express is a clone of the software and configuration used in an Engine Yard Slice. This is another amazing development. Engine Yard customers can now have a development or staging environment on their own machines that mirrors their production environment. Everyone else can simply see how powerful Engine Yard's stack of software really is. You can download Engine Yard Express , right now. Warning: the image is 343MB.
